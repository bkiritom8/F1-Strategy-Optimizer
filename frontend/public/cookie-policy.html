Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help Apex Intelligence function efficiently. They allow us to remember your race session, authenticate your access, and optimize strategy simulations.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to maintain "mission state." Without strictly necessary cookies, you would not be able to log in or save your pit strategy simulations.

3. Cookie Categories

Category Purpose Duration
Strictly Necessary Session persistence and authentication tokens. Session
Performance/Analytics Google Analytics integration to monitor platform speed. In-Memory
Operational Remembering sidebar state and UI preferences. Session

4. Managing Consent

Our platform uses an in-memory consent mechanism. This means your preferences are stored in the active browser session. If you close the browser tab or refresh, you may be asked to re-confirm your privacy protocols.

For more details on how we handle data, see our Privacy Policy.
